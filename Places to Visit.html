Places to Visit in and Around Udaipur

Must-Visit Places in Udaipur

SafarSathi4U curates the finest sightseeing experiences in Udaipur — from majestic palaces and serene lakes to hidden gems and spiritual sanctuaries.

🕌 City Palace

A majestic blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture by Lake Pichola.

  • Panoramic views of the lake and city
  • Museum with royal artifacts
  • Sound & light show in the evening

Ideal for: History lovers, couples, families

🌅 Lake Pichola

Scenic lake perfect for a romantic sunset boat ride.

  • Boat rides to Jag Mandir & Jag Niwas
  • View of palaces and temples
  • Photography paradise

Ideal for: Couples, solo travelers, photographers

🌸 Saheliyon Ki Bari

Royal garden with fountains, lotus pools, and marble elephants.

  • Lush greenery and water features
  • Peaceful walkways and pavilions

Ideal for: Nature lovers, elderly travelers

🐯 Sajjangarh (Monsoon Palace)

Hilltop palace offering breathtaking sunset views over Aravalli hills.

  • Views of Fateh Sagar Lake
  • Wildlife sanctuary nearby
  • Magical sunsets

Ideal for: Sunset lovers, adventure seekers

🛶 Fateh Sagar Lake

Calm lake with boating, cycling, and lakeside snacks.

  • Nehru Garden in the center
  • Speed & paddle boats
  • Evening strolls

Ideal for: Families, youth groups

🏞 Badi Lake

Hidden gem surrounded by hills – perfect for peaceful moments.

  • Tranquil setting
  • Beautiful sunrise/sunset

Ideal for: Offbeat explorers, nature photographers

🧘‍♂️ Shilpgram

Art & culture village showcasing Rajasthani traditions.

  • Cultural performances
  • Handicraft shopping
  • Local food stalls

Ideal for: Culture lovers, shoppers

🚙 Outskirts Day Trip

Day trips full of majestic architecture and wildlife exploration.

  • Kumbhalgarh: 2nd longest wall in the world
  • Ranakpur: 1444 intricately carved pillars

Ideal for: Explorers, spiritual seekers
